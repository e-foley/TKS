Legends Snapshots

This section is packed with over 600 full-color pictures from the show. If you're reliving your memories of the show or just want to browse around, this section should satisfy your needs.

  2. Using a limited number of low resolution images to demonstrate a television program is acceptable under copyright law.
  3. Wherever these pictures are used, you must credit Nickelodeon, Stone Stanley Inc., and their capturer (see bottom of page). You may not make a direct link to the pictures.
  4. Do not abuse this section by repeatedly loading the images. Seeing each page a handful of times is okay; however, intentionally taxing the server they're hosted on and eating up my bandwidth is not okay.
  5. These pictures will be posted as long as I am able to keep them up; however, I reserve the right to remove access to these pictures at any time for any reason. Please follow the above guidelines if you want to see these pictures in the future.

Other notes about the pictures:

  1. Some pictures have been assigned to questionable categories, but please do not ask me to change their designation.
  2. I am not currently accepting submissions from the public.
