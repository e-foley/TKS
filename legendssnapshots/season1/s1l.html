Season 1 Temple Layout Pictures

Note that the descriptions of the layouts themselves as well as the rooms inside can be found in the Temple Layouts section.

The temple layout that started it all. Source: Christopher Reynolds
The second layout the show featured, switching the Swamp to the Spider's Lair. Source: Christopher Reynolds
For the third layout, the Pirate's Cove and the Dungeon replace the Torch Room and the Mine Shaft. Source: Christopher Reynolds
Season 1's fourth layout replaces the Room of the Golden Idols with the Treasure Room. The Swamp returns, and the Throne Room is replaced by the Room of Harmonic Convergence. Source: Christopher Reynolds
The unpictured fifth layout trades the Wall Climb for the Wheel Room and the Troubled Bridge.
The sixth layout changes quite a bit of the temple. The Pirate's Cove, Dungeon, and Swamp are switched with the Room of Three Torches, the Well, and the Bamboo Forest. Source: Christopher Reynolds
Layout seven layout removes the Bamboo Forest in favor of the Holes of Pyton, and the Throne Room makes its return into the temple. Source: Christopher Reynolds
As the first season nears its end, the Room of the Three Gargoyles is replaced the Room of the Royal Gongs, and the Observatory is redone to feature celestial lights instead of the sundial. Source: Christopher Reynolds
The final layout of the first season has an unusually large number of changes in it, considering both new rooms featured were removed for the second season. The Room of Fallen Columns replaces the Heart Room, and the Treasury of Golden Orbs replaces the Treasure Room. Source: Christopher Reynolds
