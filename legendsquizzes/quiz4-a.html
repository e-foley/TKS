Very Hard Quiz Answers

You're probably thinking, "How can ANYONE have expected me to answer those questions?" This is the most difficult quiz that I can give, so you're not expected to ace it. Multiply the number of correct answers by ten to get your percentage.

Question 1:
 Name the two temple victories in which the winning team did not enter the Shrine of the Silver Monkey at any point during the run.

The correct answers are: The Mask of Shaka Zulu and the Jeweled Scabbard of Sforza

Question 2:
 Name the only episode where a temple spirit ended the team's run.

The correct answer is: The Crown of Queen Nzinga

Question 3:
 Name the only temple layout that had no victories.

The correct answer is: Season 1, Layout 5

Question 4:
 Name the only Season 2 episode that had video footage during Olmec's legend.

The correct answer is: The Missing Weathermaps of Charles Lindbergh

Question 5:
 In what season were the most victories recorded?

The correct answer is: Season 1

Can you believe it? It really seems to me that the players in those episodes never had an idea of what to do.

Question 6:
 Name the two known episodes which temple runs were taped at a different time in the season than the rest of the show.

The correct answers are: The Golden Cup of Belshazzar, and the Secret Battleplan of Nathan Hale

Question 7:
 In how many episodes was a map the artifact?

The correct answer is: Six

This was the most-hidden artifact. The episodes were, in case you were wondering, The Map to the Lost Gold Mine, Blackbeard's Treasure Map, The Treasure Map of Jean Lafitte, The Missing Weathermaps of Charles Lindbergh, The Secret Map of the Bandit Queen, and the Useless Map of the Chibcha Chieftain.

Question 8:
 Through many episodes of the first season, an gold box can be seen resting on the back-right in the Shrine of the Silver Monkey. Which episode was it featured as the artifact?

The correct answer is: The Treasure of Anne Bonny (1-9)

Question 9:
 No contestants that entered this temple room ever won the grand prize. Name this room.

The correct answer is: The Well

Question 10:
 Name the only contestant to pass THROUGH nine rooms solo before reaching the room of the artifact.

The correct answer is: Shane

Shane's incredible run was in the Very Tall Turban of Ahmed Baba (2-3)

BONUS:
 Legends won a Cable Ace award in what year?

The correct answer is: 1995

It was given out in January of 1995, most probably praising its second season.
