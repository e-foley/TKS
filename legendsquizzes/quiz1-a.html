Easy Quiz Answers

Question 1:
 How many rounds were there in the show's thirty-minute program?

The correct answer is:
C. Four

Legends always included four rounds: The Moat, the Steps of Knowledge, the Temple Games, and Olmec's Temple.

Question 2:
 Which of the following animals was NOT part of a team name?

The correct answer is:
A. Tigers

Believe it or not, the tigers were never the name of a Legends team. The teams were the Red Jaguars, the Blue Barracudas, the Green Monkeys, the Orange Iguanas, the Purple Parrots, and the Silver Snakes.

Question 3:
 In which of the following years did Legends debut?

The correct answer is:
B. 1993

Legends of the Hidden Temple debuted in September of 1993.

Question 4:
 Who was the host of Legends?

The correct answer is:
B. Kirk Fogg

Kirk Fogg was the host of Legends, while Marc Summer hosted the Double Dare series and What Would you Do?, Mike O'Malley hosted the GUTS series and Get the Picture, and Phil Moore hosted Nick Arcade and You're On!.

Question 5:
 How much time were the teams allotted while racing through the temple?

The correct answer is:
D. 3:00

That's right, a full three minutes were given to the winning team. Its members needed to use that time wisely, however, as the temple was a very difficult challenge.

BONUS:
 During Legends' three years on the air, which of the following rooms never changed name or location?

The correct answer is:
C. The Shrine of the Silver Monkey

From the first layout of Season 1 to the layout in Season 3, this room never changed at all.
