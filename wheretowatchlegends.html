Where to Watch Legends

Where can you watch such a fantastic show? Look below.

Nickelodeon GAS

Nick GAS is a network that specializes in older Nickelodeon programming, primarily game shows. As Legends of the Hidden Temple is currently one of their most popular shows, you you can expect it to be on quite often. (Hopefully, 14 times a day is enough for everyone.) Unfortunately, this channel is only available through digital cable or from a satellite dish. Dish Network currently offers Nick GaS on channel 177; however, the digital cable channel will vary from region to region. To my knowledge, the network is currently not featured by DirecTV, but I will try to update you as the situation changes.
