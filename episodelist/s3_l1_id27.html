Additional Notes:
This temple run was a very quick loss, but contrary to popular belief, it was not the quickest in the show's history. (Elizabeth the First's Golden Ship (1-2) still holds rights to that record.) After Halle started by going up to the Crypt, she was immediately caught by a temple guard who nearly took her out of the temple! She gave it her pendant, and continued onwards. She did not keep going for long, however, as a guard was also stationed in the Tomb of the Headless Kings, removing her from the temple. Then it was Jared's turn to enter. He completed the objectives in the Tomb of the Headless Kings and the Chamber of the Sacred Markers, but was taken out in the King's Storeroom to end the run. As an interesting note, there were not many episodes where a team won a single pendant. However, this is one of only two episodes in the one-pendant volume where the team was taken out on three guards. The other is The Pendant of Kamehameha (1-3).

Summary below by Der Kommissar:

[One of] the quickest loss[es] in Legends history. The first player is taken out of the temple in no time at all, and the second player gets taken out not long after. There is over a minute remaining on the clock when they lose. Side Notes: Because the loss was so soon, the end credits scroll by very slowly to make up for lost time.
