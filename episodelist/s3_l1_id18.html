Additional Notes:
It seems that most of the Season 3 episodes are either a pathetic loss or an incredible win--this one's in the incredible win column. The frontrunner was pretty fast through the rooms, and confronted her only temple guard in the Tomb of the Headless Kings. She then raced through the bottom floor, and took the passage upwards from the Dark Forest to the Shrine, where she grabbed the artifact with 1:35 to go. Her partner, Kraig, needed only to watch her finish the rest of the temple. The total time it took: One minute and forty-nine seconds, making it the second fastest completion in the history of the show.

Summary below by Der Kommissar:

This was an excellent solo temple run. The runner wasn't the smartest or fastest player in the history of the show, but it didn't make any difference - she knew what she was doing. She encountered only a single temple guard, and got the artifact out of the temple in under two minutes! Most impressive.
