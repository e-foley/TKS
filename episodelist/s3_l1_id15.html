Additional Notes:
The frontrunner, Janeen, delayed a bit through the temple, most noticably when climbing out of the Pit of the Pendulum and going to the King's Storeroom. After she was taken out by a second temple guard in the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, her partner, Jay, raced up in the temple knowing exactly what he was going to do. He raced to where his partner left off, assembled the monkey, took the ladder to the Dark Forest, found the key, and opened the door to the Jester's Court quite quickly. With the seconds ticking down, he aligned himself to the middle painting to no avail, and then tried the one on the left, also not working. He then glanced at the painting on the right and tried the center one again. This time it worked, but with only seconds remaining. He raced into the Tomb and grabbed the watch. Time expired before he could exit the room.

Summary below by Der Kommissar:

The first player was an average-skilled contestant who got thrown out of the temple in the Shrine of the Silver Monkey. Fortunately, the player who followed her was pretty fast. He went down from the Shrine into the Dark Forest, and got the watch just before time ran out.
