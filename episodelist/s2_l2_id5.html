Additional Notes:
The team generally stuck to the bottom floor of the temple, and the decision worked quite well for them. The frontrunner was taken out of the temple inside the Throne Room in no time at all, but the second contestant followed up on his progress pretty well. She found a half pendant at the left wall of the Swamp, and needed it, as there was a temple spirit residing in the following room. She made a smart decision and took the shortcut from the Dark Forest to the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, where she took an unexpected left turn. It did not really matter however, as she could not have done anything with the wasted time. It should also be mentioned that the Moat crossing was absolutely grueling--quite possibly the longest the show ever had. In fact, it ran out of its classic fog about a third of the way into the round.
