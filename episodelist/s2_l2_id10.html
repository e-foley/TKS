Additional Notes:
Plenty of temple runs had unusual finishes, but this one started off oddly. Upon running up the temple stairs, Ty dropped the team's only pendant. As he slid down into the Ledges, his partner, Jennifer, ran and picked up the pendant and then tossed it back to him just moments before a guard captured him. He then proceeded through the top floor of the temple very quickly, and grabbed the Medallion from the Mine Shaft with forty-seven seconds left. He made it out of the temple thirty-five seconds later. Although this run was a solo completion, it could not have happened without Jennifer.

Summary below by Der Kommissar:

At the beginning of the run when Ty enters the Ledges, he drops his pendant. Fortunately, his partner tossed it back to him just as a guard appeared there. He then took the high road across the temple. In the Vipers Nest, he didn't know he could just ride the elevator down, so he wasted some time there. But, he got the Medallion nonetheless and made it out of the temple before time ran out! Side Note: When the Ledges guard tries to capture Ty, the Temple Guard Capture sound effect doesn't play.
