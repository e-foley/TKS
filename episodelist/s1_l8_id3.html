Additional Notes:
The team here couldn't quite make it to their goal before their alotted three minutes. It would have helped them out greatly if they had used the door from the Tomb of Ancient Kings to the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, but instead, they used the sluggish elevator to ascend. Although it is difficult to tell from watching the show, the second player picks up a second half of a pendant on the Monkey's base in the Shrine. Also, this episode features that unusual temple game that involved sliding down a chute as far as possible, like in The Golden Jaguar of Atahualpa.

Summary below by Der Kommissar:

This temple run featured players of average skill. They probably could have had a victory, but luck was against them. They tried accessing the Treasure Room via the ladder in the Holes of Python, but it didn't open. After that, they continued onward across the bottom floor, and took the extremely-slow elevator up. The door failing to open upward to the treasure room must have discouraged them from trying to try doors leading toward the artifact. Because of this, time ran out.
