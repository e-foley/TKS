Additional Notes:
The frontrunner in this run was far too slow to be the first into the temple. He walked through most of the rooms until completing the Silver Monkey, when he picked up the pace a little bit. After descending in the elevator, he smashed through the wall, only to be taken out by the guard stationed there. Jennifer, in her attempt to make up for lost time, moved speedily though the rooms, out and around all the way to her partners removal. She found the key in the Tomb, and entered the Python Holes with a mere ten seconds remaining. Kirk commented, "I think she's gonna make it," but he spoke too soon. With three seconds left, she dove for the pearl, but her fingertips could not quite make contact with the artifact. This episode, unsurprisingly, is the closest a player has gotten to the artifact without reaching it.
