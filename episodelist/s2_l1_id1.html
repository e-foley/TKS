Additional Notes:
This was a pretty average episode. The frontrunner got taken out in the King's Storeroom, and then his partner followed his footsteps up to that point. However, after she found the key and placed it in the correct pedestal, she retreated to the Observatory, where she wasted much unnecessary time. After finding her bearings, she progressed to the Shrine of the Silver Monkey just as time ran out. This episode was quite obviously taped early in the second season for a few noticable reasons. First of all, the camerawork was much sloppier than usual, and Kirk could barely keep from announcing the temple games himself. ("And the first temple game is... Olmec, tell us about it.")
