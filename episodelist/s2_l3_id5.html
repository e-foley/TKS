Additional Notes:
There's not a whole lot to say about this episode. The team made a good effort to reach the artifact, but came up short. Like almost all other episodes, it would have helped the team out if they had taken the Shrine/Dark Forest passage instead of the long way. Also, if the second player had run up directly to the Crypt after the frontrunner was removed from the temple, that would have saved time as well. As a final note: If the team managed to reach the Ivory Elephant, they would have visited all twelve rooms getting there.
