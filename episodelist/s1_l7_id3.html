Additional Notes:
I can't say a whole lot about this episode, except to say that the contestants moved much, much too slowly in the temple. By watching the run, you could definitely tell that Kirk felt the same way. The team ran out of time in the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, two rooms away from the artifact. Note that the player picked up a second half-pendant of life in the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, but it was completely unannounced by Kirk Fogg. (Thanks to "The Ancient Warrior" for this information.)

Contributed Summaries and Notes:

Der Kommissar: Here's one of those temple runs where taking the path directly to the artifact fails, and it costs the team dearly. The first runner encountered a Temple Guard at the Cave Of Size, but continued over to the [Room of Harmonic Convergence] and up to the Observatory, where a second guard took her out. Her partner followed her footsteps, but wasted too much time in the Treasure Room. His time ran out in the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, as he was putting the statue together. Side Notes: This episode marks the only time an artifact is ever hidden in the Bamboo Forest. It's also interesting to note that the episode only gets the room's name right ONCE in the entire episode (Olmec's summary of the temple.) The room is referred to as the Bamboo Swamp when Olmec says where the artifact his hidden, and as the Swamp by Kirk. Also, the arm is on the ledge at the front of the room when its location is revealed prior to the Steps of Knowledge, but it is tangled in the mass of bamboo strands during the temple run.
