Additional Notes:
This is said to be the first episode that ever aired on Nickelodeon. Well, the crew chose a good one to show, as this was the most exciting one-second win in the first season. The frontrunner wasn't too fast, but seemed to know what she was doing. After she was removed from the Room of Fallen Columns, her partner raced toward where she left off. He progressed through the rooms more quickly than his partner did, and made it all the way to the Holes of Python, where the artifact was hidden. It looked doubtful up to this point that the team would win, but after he grabbed the Headdress with 0:28 seconds left, he went into high gear. He blazed through the Wheel Room and the Cave of Size, and made it through the temple gates with but a second to go.
