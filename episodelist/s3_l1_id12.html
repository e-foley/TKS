Additional Notes:
The Red Jaguars would have had a much better chance of winning if they took the path downwards from the Shrine instead of going around the horn. Unfortunately, this is a mistake that comes too often, and it really cost them here. One small note: If I'm not mistaken, the players still have to shout out the correct password when entering from the Shrine to open the Pharoah's Secret Passage, right? The frontrunner didn't do it here.

Summary below by Michael Hackney:

That printing plate, while plastic, was huge! Imagine if one of the two players had gotten to the plate. That would've been a fight trying to get through the Pit, as they would've not only been trying not to fall over the foam blocks, but also trying not to trip over the plate itself. Going through the Ledges might've been an even worse fight, considering how tight it was in there.
