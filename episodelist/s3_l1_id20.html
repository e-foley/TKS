Additional Notes:
It seemed like the Legends crew had a great time coming up with unique layouts in the third season. This temple design was one of those. The side door of the Crypt didn't open after Jared pulled all three books, so he was forced to traverse downwards into the Ledges. Here, only the bottom door leading to the Pit of the Pendulum opened. As he progressed, he was forced to push the column down with his own hands to continue. After he did this, the door to the Tomb of the Headless Kings did not open, so he climbed up the wall and took the stairway to the King's Storeroom. He then followed the top path to the Shrine. After he assembled the monkey, the door directly to the Room of the Secret Password did not open, so he took the ladder down to the Dark Forest. He had a moment of hesitation, but he smashed through the wall to the Quicksand Bog and got caught by his second temple guard. His partner, Katie, followed exactly where her partner went, and had about forty seconds to make some progress. She made a smart move and took the ladder from the Shrine to the Quicksand Bog, but time ran out before she could exit the room. I really wanted to see this team win; It's too bad that they came up short. A final note about the episode: After the Green Monkeys swept the temple games and prevented the Silver Snakes from scoring, Kirk said, "Great sportsmanship by the Silver Monkeys," and then continued to give his spiel about how they had a great gift for the team.
