Additional Notes:
This was a pitiful temple run. The frontrunner was quite slow, and additionally had very bad luck. After raising the slab in the Wheel Room, the door behind it was locked. She retreated back to the Cave of Sighs and went up to the Room of the Three Gargoyles. In here, she pressed on all three tongues, but the door to the left didn't budge. After taking a long time forcing open the door, she made her way to the Heart Room with a minute left. Instead of looking for the open door down into the Throne Room, she went backwards to the Troubled Bridge again, where time ran out. I expected MUCH more out of this team, as they had won two pendants!
