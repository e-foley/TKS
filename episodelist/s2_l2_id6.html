Additional Notes:
This is one of my favorite episodes ever. The Blue Barracudas as a team were a great pair, excelling at the Steps of Knowlege and winning the temple games (with a single pendant), but in the end, it was only one Blue Barracuda that made the difference. Little did she know, Missy would set a Legends record. She raced into the temple, and slid down the slide into the Ledges. From here, she followed the bottom path all the way to the Dark Forest, where she immediately went upwards, and continued along the top floor directly to the artifact. She raced into Medusa's Lair, grabbed the pillow, and darted back out, climbing past the Pit and through the Ledges until she raced up the final path behind Olmec, and then down the stairs to record the incredible solo win with twenty-one whole seconds to spare. After finishing the run, she became so joyous that she nearly cried, hugging her partner and Kirk as a celebration for her win. All her nervous energy throughout the show paid off! What record did she break, you might ask? Well, Missy was the first player ever to retrieve an artifact from the center room of the temple, and she did the whole task solo! Kirk's enthusiasm towards the temple run was additionally quite memorable. Missy deserved her trip to Jamaica.
