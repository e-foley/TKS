Additional Notes:
After the frontrunner, Robert, is taken out on two consecutive temple guards, the second player races in and reaches the Viper's Nest. It takes a while for Olivia-Emma to find the amulet, but once she does, she blazes back. With the seconds ticking down, she drops into the pit, and then climbs into the Ledges with ten seconds remaining. Once she finally climbs through them, four seconds remain, and she desperately runs towards the temple gate. The camera switches to a wide-angle view, and just as the final second comes, a jumping body emerges from the temple entrance and lands on the ground, recording the closest victory in the show's history. The team passed up an obvious half pendant at the entrance to Medusa's Lair, but it made no difference. This is, in my opinion, one of the best episodes ever produced (even if the temple design was much simpler than usual).

Two interesting notes: The Blue Barracudas were denied advancing to the Steps of Knowelege because a spotter accedentally sent the Silver Snake team back by mistake. Also, Olivia-Emma was also on the Nick game show Get the Picture before her appearance on Legends (or so I hear).
