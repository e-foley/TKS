Additional Notes:
This is yet another episode where the team has difficulty reaching the artifact at the center of the temple. The frontrunner was forced to zig-zag up and down at the beginning of the run, which cost the team some time. When the frontrunner reached the pendulum rope, she did not know how to get on. She made a miserable attempt, swung, and fell from the pendulum before it could reach the column. She wasted some time trying to climb back up the walls to try again before she realized she could just push over the column with her hands and continue. This is one of the few episodes where a player pushes over the column and does not use the pendulum to do so. The frontrunner found her second temple guard in the Quicksand bog, and her partner then had to retrace her steps with under a minute to go. He made it to the Pharaoh's Secret Passage when time ran out, having completed no room objectives. Note: The second player gained access to the door leading from the Dark Forest to the Shrine of the Silver Monkey, but did not use it to ascend.

Summary below by Ian Fisne:

The first player pointed out the half-pendant to her partner; it was in the Jester's Court on the top button of the middle wall painting. (You have to look closely to see it; look for the black hanging strap and then the moon-shaped half pendant piece below it.) I don't think the second partner saw her signaling, plus after she opened up the room it became dark, he wouldn't have seen his half-pendant anyway. Unfortunately, even if the players had sped up more, the second runner probably still would have been taken out by the third guard; without that half-pendant, the only way the Green Monkeys could have won was if the third guard was in the King's Storeroom, and the door from the Ancient Warriors room to the Chamber opened. This was a tough layout, and, without 2 pendants, it ultimately came down to luck. To the best of my knowledge, this was the only time a half-pendant was hidden in the Jester's Court.
