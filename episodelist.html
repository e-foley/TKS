Episode list Notes:
  • The episodes are listed in the order which their temple runs were taped, as precisely as the Legends fanbase knows it.
  • Click a season heading to see all of the outcomes of the episodes in that season.
  • There are numerous cases where there are alternate spellings of a title possible.
